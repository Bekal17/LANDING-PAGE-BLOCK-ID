What makes BlockID different?

Traditional identity systems require KYC, personal data, and centralized trust. BlockID builds identity from on-chain behavior — what you do, not who you are. Your identity is owned by you as an NFT, not by any corporation.

Trust Score

Every wallet on BlockID gets a behavioral trust score powered by AI. This score represents how trustworthy a wallet is based entirely on its on-chain activity — no personal data, no KYC.

74

Behavioral Trust Score

Score ranges from 0 (high risk) to 100 (highly trusted). Updated in real-time as new on-chain activity is detected.

How It Works

The Trust Score is generated by a multi-stage AI pipeline that analyzes your wallet's complete on-chain history on Solana:

  • Transaction Pattern Analysis — frequency, timing, volume, and diversity of transactions
  • Counterparty Network — who you transact with and their trust levels
  • Graph Clustering — identifying wallet networks and relationships
  • Flow Analysis — tracing SOL and token movements for suspicious patterns
  • Drainer Detection — known scam and drainer pattern recognition
  • DEX/CEX Behavior — routing habits across exchanges and protocols

NFT Identity

When you connect your wallet to BlockID, we automatically mint a soul-bound NFT as your on-chain identity. This NFT is non-transferable — it's yours forever.

What's in Your Identity NFT?

  • Trust Score — your current behavioral score, updated on-chain
  • @Handle — your claimed name (if any)
  • Wallet Address — the linked Solana wallet
  • Metadata — risk level, scoring date, identity creation timestamp

NFT Avatar & Banner

On Explorer and Pro plans, you can mint your own NFT from any image (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP) and set it as your profile avatar or banner. Your NFT avatar gets a distinctive gold border visible across the platform.

Soul-bound = Sybil Resistant

Because Identity NFTs are non-transferable, they can't be bought, sold, or faked. This makes BlockID inherently resistant to sybil attacks — every identity represents real on-chain behavior, not purchased credentials.

Technical Details

  • Built on Metaplex Core (Solana's NFT standard)
  • Metadata stored on-chain + Cloudflare R2 for media
  • NFT images fetched via Helius DAS API for fast rendering
  • Auto-minted on first login — no gas fee for the user

Behavioral AI

BlockID's core innovation: building identity from behavior, not personal data. Our AI analyzes on-chain transaction patterns to create a behavioral fingerprint — unique to you, without KYC or doxxing.

What Gets Analyzed

  • Transaction Patterns — frequency, timing, volume, and token diversity
  • Routing Behavior — which DEXs, CEXs, and protocols you interact with
  • Counterparty Analysis — the trust level of wallets you transact with
  • Network Graph — cluster analysis of related wallets and communities
  • Temporal Patterns — timing consistency and behavioral regularity

The Pipeline

scoring-pipeline.txt 
  Solana Transactions (Helius RPC)
          ↓
  Graph Clustering          — identify wallet networks
          ↓
  Flow Analysis             — trace SOL/token movement
          ↓
  Drainer Detection         — detect known scam patterns
          ↓
  Evidence Collector        — attach tx proof (Solscan links)
          ↓
  Reason Aggregator         — collect reason codes
          ↓
  ML Wallet Scoring         — RandomForest model (0–100)
          ↓
  PostgreSQL Storage        — persist scores + metadata
          ↓
  Identity NFT Update       — reflect on-chain
Privacy by Design

BlockID never collects personal information. No email, no name, no KYC. Your identity is built entirely from public on-chain data — the same data anyone can see on a block explorer. We just make it useful.
