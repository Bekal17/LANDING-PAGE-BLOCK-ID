Five composable primitives that make blockchain accessible to everyone, not just developers and traders.

@Handle — Your Universal Name

Claim your unique @handle and link it to your Solana wallet. One name, works across every app on blockchain. Not just Solana — your identity, everywhere.

Profile & Social Layer

Post, follow, and connect with other wallets. Build your on-chain reputation through real interactions — not just transaction history.

Trust Score

Every wallet gets a behavioral trust score powered by AI. See who you can trust before you transact — no KYC, no doxxing, just on-chain signals.

Behavioral AI

BlockID analyzes on-chain transaction patterns to build your behavioral identity — no KYC, no personal data. Your reputation is built from what you do on-chain, not who you are.
